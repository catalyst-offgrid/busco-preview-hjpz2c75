03 — MECHANICS

From standby to active.
In four phases.

Pre-succession
Standby

Triggers, mandates, and step-in rights defined. Data pipelines validated.

II.
Activation
Transition

Pre-agreed triggers hit: insolvency, covenant breach, PAR threshold.

III.
Live
Active

IV.
Resolution

One BUS.

TOMORROW

The path to scale. Securitization unlocks institutional capital. A robust BUS is a prerequisite for institutional investors.
